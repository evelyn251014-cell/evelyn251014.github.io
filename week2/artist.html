Lalisa Manoban

Lalisa Manoban, also known as Lisa. She is a member of the K-pop girl group BLACKPINK. She was born on March 27, 1997 in Buriram, Thailand. She is known for her sharp dance skills and powerful stage presence.

Lisa trained for several years in South Korea under YG Entertainment before debuting with BLACKPINK in 2016. In 2021, she made her solo debut with the single album LALISA which gained massive popularity and set new streaming records. Besides music, she is also a fasion icon and works with famous international brands such as Louis Vuitton, Bulgari and Nike.
