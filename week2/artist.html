Lalisa Manoban

Biography

Lalisa Manoban, also known as Lisa, is a famous Thai singer, rapper, and dancer. She was born on March 27, 1997, in Buriram, Thailand. Lisa is a member of the popular K-pop girl group BLACKPINK. She is well known for her strong stage presence, sharp dance moves, and confident personality. Many fans admire her talent and hardworking attitude.

Lisa moved to South Korea at a young age after passing an audition with YG Entertainment. She trained for several years before making her debut with BLACKPINK in 2016. The group quickly became one of the biggest girl groups in the world. In 2021, Lisa released her first solo single album titled LALISA, which became very successful and broke many records.

Besides music, Lisa is also a fashion icon. She works with famous international brands such as Louis Vuitton, Bulgari, and Nike. Today, Lisa is loved by millions of fans around the world for her talent, beauty, and dedication.
